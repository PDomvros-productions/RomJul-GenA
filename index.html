AFK (Gaming) "Away From Keyboard." Someone who is not present or distracted.
Asset (Gaming) An object, sound, or item in a video game file. Used here to mean a physical part of a person (like a hand/foot).
Aura / Aura Points (Slang) A mystical energy or "cool factor." You gain aura by doing cool things, lose it by doing embarrassing things.
Baddie (Slang) A very attractive and confident person (usually female).
Banned (Gaming) Blocked from the server. Used here to mean "name is forbidden."
Bet (Slang) "Yes," "Okay," or "I agree."
Boss Fight (Gaming) A difficult challenge or enemy at the end of a level.
Build (Gaming) The specific combination of skills/items a character has. Romeo says he will "respawn as a new build" (change his identity).
Camping (Gaming) Staying in one spot (usually hidden) for a long time to ambush others.
CEO of... (Slang) The boss or master of something (e.g., "CEO of my heart").
Clip Into (Gaming) To glitch through a solid object (like a wall) that you shouldn't be able to pass through.
Cooked (Slang) To be defeated, doomed, or in big trouble. Also "roasted" (insulted).
Dev (Gaming) Developer. The creators/admins of the game (gods).
Down Bad / Down Catastrophic (Slang) Desperately in love or obsessed with someone. "Catastrophic" is an extreme version.
Drip (Slang) Extremely fashionable outfit or style.
Fanum Tax (Slang) To steal a portion of someone's food (popularized by streamer Fanum). Used here to mean "take/remove."
Finna (AAVE/Slang) "Fixing to" or "Going to."
Firewall (Tech) Security system. Used here to represent the orchard walls.
FOMO (Slang) Fear Of Missing Out.
Gigachad (Meme) The ultimate masculine, perfect man.
Glitch (Gaming) A bug in the system. Used here as "moving unexpectedly" or "bypassing walls."
Glow Up (Slang) An incredible transformation in appearance.
Goated (Slang) G.O.A.T. (Greatest of All Time). Being the best.
God-mode (Gaming) A cheat code that makes you invincible/cannot take damage.
Grind (Gaming) To work hard repetitively to achieve a goal.
Handle / Gamertag (Tech) Username or online identity.
Hard Launch (Social Media) Suddenly revealing a relationship to the public with a serious photo (opposite of "soft launch" where you hide their face).
Hitbox (Gaming) The invisible box around a character where they can take damage. Romeo refers to his body as his "hitbox."
L / Take an L (Slang) "Loss." A failure or embarrassment.
Lagging (Gaming) Moving slowly or freezing due to bad internet connection.
Lazy-loading (Tech) When images/clouds only load when you get close to them to save memory.
Lobby (Gaming) The waiting area before a game starts. Used here to mean "the universe/sky."
Locking In (Slang) Getting hyper-focused and serious.
Lore (Slang) The backstory or history of a character.

Mid (Slang) Mediocre, average, or low quality.
Mogging (Slang) Being significantly better looking or more dominant than someone else.
No Cap (Slang) "No lie." Truth.
NPC (Gaming) Non-Player Character. Someone who can't think for themselves or is just background decoration.
Opp (Slang) Opposition/Enemy.
Permadeath (Gaming) Permanent death. If you die, you cannot respawn.
Pookie (Slang) Cute nickname for a partner.

Reroll (Gaming) To restart or generate a new character/username to get better stats.
